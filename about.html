A little about me

I move between different worlds: finance, technology and travel. Always curious about how they connect and how thoughtful experiences can shape the way people live.

By day, I build products and customer experiences in banking. Beyond work, I explore places, collect stories and create small projects that turn everyday moments into something more meaningful.

This space is where those worlds come together.

What I do

Over the past few years, I've built engine, designed payment experiences and launched programs that help people engage more seamlessly with financial products.

I'm particularly interested in how technology, human behavior, and design can create experiences that feel simple, intuitive and valuable.

Outside of work

You'll usually find me planning my next trip, discovering cafes, playing tennis, pilates or turning travel memories into creative projects. I believe we don't have to become just one thing.

I'm learning to embrace different interests, collect experiences from many places and bloom beyond a single path.
